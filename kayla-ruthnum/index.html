WELCOME TO THE HOLIDAY HOME

At our luxurious holiday home, you can experience a truly relaxing getaway surrounded by nature. Situated in a beautiful park with spectacular views of the lake and mountain, it’s the perfect place to unwind and reconnect with yourself. The accommodation is fully equipped with high-end amenities such as wifi, a pool, and an outdoor play area for kids to enjoy. Indulge yourself in one of our gorgeous rooms while admiring the breath taking view outside your window. Unwind after a long day of exploring with a cocktail at the relaxing lounge and enjoy the self catering options available. Come stay with us and create lasting memories that will have you coming back time and again!
